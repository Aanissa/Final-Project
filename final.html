Our Goal

Our goal while programming this website is to give students in the New York Area a resource to find safe volunteer opportunities. This website will reduce the time it takes to find the right opportunity for each student. By using our NYConnect opportunity finder, students will be guided to the best volunteer job for them. We have researched and verfied all of our orgainzations to make sure the matches are safe, because a volunteer's own wellbeing is priority. We hope that by using our website, students will be led to the best opportunities for them.
